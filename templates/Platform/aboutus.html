About Us



Our Mission

Navsanjeevan is a non-profit organization that aims to provide education to the underprivileged children of the society. We believe that education is the most powerful weapon which can be used to change the world. We are a group of people who are passionate about education and want to make a difference in the society. We are working towards providing education to the children who are not able to afford it. We are also working towards providing them with the basic necessities of life. We believe that every child has the right to education and we are working towards making this a reality.
